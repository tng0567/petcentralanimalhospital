Pet Central Animal Hospital

Welcome to our caring community, where your pets' health and happiness are our top priority. We're here to support you with expert veterinary care, ensuring your furry family members thrive.

Explore what we have to offer

Veterinary Hospital

Our caring team offers high-quality services, from routine check-ups to advanced treatments, in a welcoming environment

Surgery

Providing a state-of-the-art surgical suite that is equipped with the latest technology and advanced tools.

Boarding

Offering year-round, weekend, and holiday boarding services.

Grooming

Pet Central is also the home of Dirty Dog and Cat Grooming, a full-service grooming and a self-service pet wash.

Who we are

Pet Central Animal Hospital is a full service animal hospital that welcomes both emergency treatment cases as well as patients in need of routine medical, surgical, and dental care. Our veterinarians have years of experience treating serious conditions and offering regular pet wellness care.
